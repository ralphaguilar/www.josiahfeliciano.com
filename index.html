Josiah Kyle Feliciano - Film Producer

About me:

I am a versatile film producer who has interned for the MCIAT studio in San Diego, where I handled and managed the company’s library system while also tending to other clerical tasks. In terms of school, I have produced four short films, three of which were selected at the SDSU Emerging Filmmakers Showcase. I also have experience with producing documentaries; I co-produced two while producing some of the aforementioned short films. All in all, I am a strong project manager who excels at multitasking and meeting deadlines.

I have graduated from San Diego State with a B.S. in Film, Television, and New Media with an emphasis in production. I specialize in producing and have an unconventional passion for alleviating the workloads of my colleagues.
